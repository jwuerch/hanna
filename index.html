Meet the Doctors

Mark A. Hanna D.M.D.

Dr. Hanna is a unique small town dentist. His extensive focus on postdoctoral study of clinical dentistry places him in the top 5% of all dentists for continuing education, enabling him to provide some of the latest advances in dental technology—things like digital x-rays, Hybridge Restorations, and E4D same-day porcelain restorations.

Best of all, Dr. Hanna combines this “big city” clinical dentistry with “small town” care and communication that will make you feel right at home. You won’t get any snooty lectures here, just clear, understandable explanations and the latest in advanced dentistry. It’s the best of both worlds, and it really is something to smile about!

Member of: American Dental Association, Academy General Dentistry, Massachusetts Dental Society, American Academy of Implant Dentistry, International Congress of Oral Implantologists

Gregory L. Paskerian D.M.D., M.A.G.D., F.I.C.D.

Doctor Gregory Paskerian is a different kind of dentist. His use of advanced dental technology combines with a gentle style that will make you and your family more comfortable than you ever thought possible.

A doctoral graduate of the prestigious Tufts University, School of Dental Medicine, Dr. Paskerian continues to share his knowledge, as a clinical instructor focusing on implant dentistry, and cosmetic and restorative dentistry. Yet, despite his extensive focus on clinical dentistry, Dr. Paskerian takes the time to get to know each and every patient one on one, helping you make informed choices about your dental health.

Member of: International College of Dentists, Academy of General Dentistry, American Dental Association, International Congress of Oral Implantologists, American Equilabration Society, Northeast Prosthodontic Society, American Academy of Implant Dentistry, L.D. Pankey Alumni Association
